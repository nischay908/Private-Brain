✦ How It Works

Privacy by architecture, not policy.

The AI model runs inside your browser tab using WebGPU. Your data literally cannot leave — there's no server to send it to.

01

Load the App

Open PrivateBrain in Chrome or Edge. The app downloads a 600MB AI model to your browser cache once.

02

Model Loads Locally

Llama 3.2 1B runs fully in your browser tab via WebGPU — no backend, no API calls, no accounts.

03

You Work Privately

Chat, write, analyze documents. Everything is processed on your CPU/GPU. Nothing leaves your machine.

04

Data Stays Yours

All history, notes, and chats are saved in your browser's localStorage. Delete anytime. No accounts needed.
